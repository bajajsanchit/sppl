    Our Products

    Sudarshan Polyfab with its motto “Quality makes a Statement” aims to serve the domestic and global markets and bring about a revolutionary change in packaging industry by enhancing value, life and brand of customer’s products. We offer versatile packaging solutions like BOPP bags (with/ without clear window), flexographically printed woven bags (laminated/ unlaminated), woven bags with centre/ shifted gusseting options, woven bags with various cutting options (like zig-zag cut, heat cut and blade cut), bags with anti-skid, perforation and holes, bags with various handle options and FIBC bags (in various shapes, sizes and spout options).

    Our Target Sectors

    The demand for Raffia packaging material is directly related to economic growth, agricultural production, industrial production and trade, as goods are produced and then it is bulk distributed both locally and globally. Today, India is the second largest producer of woven sacks and largest exporter of Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers (FIBCs).
